The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum located in Agra, India, that was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his favorite wife, Mumtaz Mahal.

The Brandenburg Gate is a historic neoclassical monument in Berlin, Germany, which serves as a symbol of the tumultuous history of Europe and Germany.

The Eiffel Tower is an iconic iron lattice tower located on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France.
